About Gurgaon International City

A New Benchmark in Luxury Township Living

Gurgaon International City represents the pinnacle of integrated township development in the rapidly growing Manesar corridor. Strategically positioned in Sector 9, 10, 11, this prestigious project by M3M India offers a harmonious blend of residential excellence, commercial vibrancy, and lifestyle amenities.

Spread across 140+ acres, GIC features meticulously planned residential floors, premium plots, and modern SCO spaces. Every aspect of this township has been designed to provide residents with an unparalleled living experience that combines comfort, convenience, and contemporary aesthetics.
