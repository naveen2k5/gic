What is M3M GIC Manesar?

M3M GIC Manesar is a premium integrated township offering luxury residential floors, plots, and SCO spaces in Sector 3, Manesar. Developed by M3M India, it features world-class amenities and strategic connectivity to major business hubs and transportation networks.

Where is M3M GIC Manesar located?

M3M GIC Manesar is strategically located in Sector 3, Manesar, Haryana. It offers excellent connectivity to Dwarka Expressway (5 minutes), NH-8, IGI Airport (30 minutes), and major business districts in Gurugram and Delhi.

What types of properties are available at M3M GIC Manesar?

The project offers three main property types: luxury residential floors (3BHK & 4BHK independent floors), premium residential plots (150-500 sq. yards), and SCO (Shop-Cum-Office) spaces for commercial purposes. Each category is designed to cater to different lifestyle and investment needs.

What amenities are available at M3M GIC Manesar?

M3M GIC Manesar features over 40 world-class amenities including a premium clubhouse, swimming pool, fully-equipped gymnasium, landscaped gardens, sports facilities (badminton, basketball, tennis), kids' play areas, 24/7 security with CCTV surveillance, power backup, wellness center, and ample parking spaces.
